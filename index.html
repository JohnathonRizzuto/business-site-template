Our Story

A Real Barbershop. Not a Chain.

Exclusive Fades sits in the heart of downtown Plano, right on South Center. We built this shop on one promise — you walk in scruffy, you walk out sharp. Every time.

Whether you're a regular for Mario's skin fades, here for one of Myles' beard sculpts, or it's your kid's first chair — we treat every cut like it's the one you'll remember.
