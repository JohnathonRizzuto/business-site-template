Our Story

A Real Barbershop. The Way It Used To Be.

Ron's has been a fixture on North Main for as long as folks around here can remember. No flashy gimmicks, no $80 cuts. Just clean classic haircuts, hot beard work, and good conversation while you wait.

We've cut three generations of Sandwich men — grandfathers, fathers, and the next round of boys with their first real haircut. We don't take ourselves too seriously, but we take your cut seriously.
